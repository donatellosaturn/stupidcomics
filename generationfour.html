Generation 4 focuses on the aftermath of an intense battle between four magic-wielders and the dubious Mr. Time.
Those heroes trapped him in a limbo-like state, however, sacrificing their souls or their powers in the process.
Their mystic powers had been locked in gemstones, scattered across the four corners of the world.
However, suddenly, a shady organization who calls themselves the Twilight Organization plot to revive Mr. Time by finding the four gemstones.
Will the last-remaining magic wielder of the original four and his protige be able to defeat them?
